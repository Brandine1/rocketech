Cases

A High-Quality, Fast and Secure Crypto Mining Pool

Sigmapool is a scalable crypto mining pool with 60,000+ active workers and 700+ miners, offering a secure environment for Blockchain and Litecoin mining. With an ongoing blog, hardware store and real-time updates, it provides a seamless experience even under heavy workloads.

The World's First Chess Gambling Strategy

Dice Chess is a timeless chess classic with a 25-year history given a new life with elements of a game of chance. Users deposit money and compete with other players but have to roll the dice to determine which piece they may move. The game can handle over 10,000 requests per minute across 15+ screen types.

Transforming Legal Education with Ginger Snail

Ginger Snail is a cutting-edge educational app designed for iOS users. With its primary focus on law students and legal professionals, it offers a comprehensive platform that combines interactive tutorials and convenient knowledge assessment tools. By seamlessly integrating technology into legal education, the app revolutionizes the learning experience, providing a competitive edge over traditional paper books.

All-in-One Online Events Platform

Everytale is a powerful instrument for creating, promoting and broadcasting remarkable virtual experiences — conferences, summits, webinars, workshops and online courses. With features like flexible settings, live broadcasts, a recommendation system, gamification elements and content promotion tools, the platform ensures seamless event organization, engagement and monetization.

The World's First Matchmaking and Combat Sports Streaming Platform

Spartacus is a matchmaking and streaming platform where combat sports fighters can participate in tournaments, increase their rating and receive rewards. Developed by a team of 15 specialists, Spartacus seamlessly merges web and mobile platforms, catering to the needs of both PC users and mobile enthusiasts.
